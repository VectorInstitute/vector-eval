The PSLV-C56 is the 58th mission of Indian Space Research Organisation's Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) and the 17th flight of the PSLV-CA variant, and will be get launched from Satish Dhawan Space Centre First Launch Pad ( FLP ).

Launch
It is Scheduled to get launched on Sunday, 30 July 2023 at 06:30 IST / 01:00 UTC from Satish Dhawan Space Centre, Sriharikota, Andhra Pradesh, India. This is a dedicated commercial mission through NSIL with DS-SAR as primary satellite and VELOX-AM as a co-passenger satellite With other 5 Satellites, All satellites from this mission belongs to Singapore.
